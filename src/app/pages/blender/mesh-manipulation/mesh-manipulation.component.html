General Mesh Manipulation

Combining Vertices

  • Method 1.
    1. Click vertices
    2. Press M to merge
    3. Merge at center
  • Method 2.
    1. Go to Tools
    2. Options
    3. Automerge
    4. Turn Snap on > Set to Vertex
    5. Press G twice to slide vertex to the other vertices

Extrude Along Normals

  1. Alt + E
  2. Extrude Along Normals

Joining Objects

  1. Shift Click 2 objects (The order is important, last object is the destination object)
  2. Press E

Seperating Objects

  1. Select Faces or Press L to select the whole object
  2. Press P > Seperate by selection

Select Similiar

  1. Select an edge
  2. Shift + G
  3. Crease, Seam, Sharpness

Fix Circle Merged at Center

  1. Select the weird faces
  2. Dissolve Edges
  3. Poke Face

Apply All modifiers

  1. Select All (Press A)
  2. Open search menu with Space or F3 and search "Convert to Mesh"
  3. Or access from the Menu > Object > Convert to > Mesh
